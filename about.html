Hi, I’m Muhammad Ali Ghias

I am a highly motivated Software Engineering student, Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador Lead at CUSIT, and an award-winning Graphic Designer with a strong passion for technology, design, and innovation. With a unique blend of technical and creative expertise, I specialize in web development, frontend projects, UI/UX design, and digital content creation.

Currently, I am leading the Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors chapter at CUSIT, where I mentor and guide students to explore Microsoft technologies, develop technical skills, and contribute to community projects. I previously served as the Graphic Designing Lead at CGBZ (CUSIT E-Gaming Battle Zone), where I conceptualized and designed logos, posters, banners, and social media content, contributing to brand growth and engaging digital campaigns.

As a freelancer, I have delivered creative design solutions for multiple clients, including logos, flyers, brochures, posters, and social media campaigns, combining aesthetic appeal with functional design. I hold certifications from Coursera, Udemy, Mind Luster, and over 100 Microsoft badges, reflecting my commitment to continuous learning and professional development.

My approach to work revolves around problem-solving, creativity, and impact, whether I am developing a responsive web application, designing a visual identity, or mentoring fellow students. I thrive at the intersection of technology and design, constantly exploring new tools, frameworks, and techniques to deliver high-quality, innovative solutions.

In everything I do, I aim to blend creativity with logic, inspire others through learning and collaboration, and contribute meaningfully to both technological and creative communities.
